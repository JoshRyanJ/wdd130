Featured Trips

Big Oaks Adventure

Duration: Full Day (6-7 hours) | Difficulty: Moderate | Price: $159 per person

Experience the thrill of navigating Class III rapids through the stunning Big Oaks Canyon. This full-day adventure includes a riverside lunch, all safety equipment, and plenty of opportunities to swim in calmer sections. Perfect for adventurous families and groups looking for an exciting but manageable challenge. Our expert guides will navigate you through exciting rapids while sharing fascinating facts about the local wildlife and geology.

Highlights: Scenic canyon views, swimming holes, wildlife spotting, riverside lunch included

Minimum Age: 10 years old

Rapid Dips Extreme

Duration: Half Day (4 hours) | Difficulty: Advanced | Price: $189 per person

For the ultimate adrenaline rush, take on the legendary Rapid Dips section featuring intense Class IV-V rapids. This trip is designed for experienced rafters seeking heart-pounding action and technical challenges. You'll tackle massive waves, steep drops, and tight maneuvers that will test your skills and leave you breathless. This is not for the faint of heart!

Highlights: Extreme rapids, technical challenges, experienced guides, waterproof photo package available

Minimum Age: 16 years old, previous rafting experience required

Mild Movements Family Float

Duration: Half Day (3 hours) | Difficulty: Easy | Price: $89 per person

Perfect for first-time rafters and families with young children, this gentle float trip offers all the fun of rafting without the intensity of big rapids. Enjoy Class I-II rapids that provide just enough excitement for younger adventurers while allowing plenty of time to relax and take in the beautiful scenery. This trip includes frequent stops for swimming, splash fights, and nature exploration.

Highlights: Family-friendly, gentle rapids, swimming stops, nature education, snacks included

Minimum Age: 5 years old (with parent/guardian)

Palm Spring Waters

Duration: Full Day (8 hours) | Difficulty: Moderate-Advanced | Price: $199 per person

This spectacular full-day journey takes you through some of the most beautiful and diverse river sections in our area. Starting with moderate Class II-III rapids in the morning, the afternoon intensifies with challenging Class IV rapids that will get your heart racing. Includes a delicious riverside BBQ lunch, all equipment, and waterproof bags for your belongings. This trip offers the best combination of scenery and excitement!

Highlights: All-day adventure, diverse rapids, riverside BBQ lunch, stunning scenery, guided nature walk

Minimum Age: 12 years old
